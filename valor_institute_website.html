About Blake

Built on
Faith & Iron.

For most of my life I was capable — but capable of what, exactly? I had drive without direction. Strength without purpose. I could handle whatever life threw at me, as long as nobody asked me what I was building toward.

When I stopped hiding from God and came into the Catholic Church, everything reordered. I finally had a standard outside of myself — one that didn't move based on how I felt that morning. Truth. Order. Purpose.

That discipline built more than a body. It built Philosophically Built — a gym I founded and still own and operate today. A staple of our community. And now through Valor Institute, I'm putting everything I've learned into the hands of men who are ready.
